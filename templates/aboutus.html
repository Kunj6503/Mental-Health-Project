MENTAL HEALTH FOR EVERYONE

Neuralife is an India-based open source webapp dedicated for testing depression. Neuralife allows people to test depression on this webapp, which are then predicted by a machine-learning algorithm. In Neuralife we help people to overcome their depression so that they can live in peace.
